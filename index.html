  • The Competition

    ICPC is an annual algorithmic programming competition where teams of three college students compete to solve as many challenging programming problems as they can within 5 hours. ICPC is separated into two stages: regional and world finals. The top 3 teams in each region advance to the world finals, which are held in a different country each year.


  • The Group

    SIGICPC is for anyone who likes competitive programming, but is particularly targeted towards those who want to do well in the competition. We meet every week to practice solving challenging problems. Being able to solve challenging problems is particularly helpful for technical interviews as well.

    In the past 10 years, our school has advanced to world finals 8 times.
